Chinese Alumni Association

加州大学圣克鲁兹分校中国校友会始建于2018年，经过UCSC校友会和UC Santa Cruz 中国学生学者联谊会的帮助，校友会寻求更好的方式连结、服务并支持所有UCSC的中国校友。

校友会致力于在世界范围内帮助和发展校友之间的关系和社交范围，发展校友精神，同时帮助学校在中国扩大影响力。校友会也将成为UC Santa Cruz校友与在校学生的桥梁，互相支持、共同进步。

我们衷心欢迎所有UC Santa Cruz的中国校友成为我们的一份子！

The UC Santa Cruz Chinese Alumni Association (CAA) was founded in September, 2018. With the help of the Alumni Association and the Chinese Students and Scholars Association, CAA seeks better ways to reach, serve, and engage and all Chinese alumni of UC Santa Cruz, help improve the network among Chinese alumni and develop the relationship between UC Santa Cruz community and Chinese alumni community.

The CAA is dedicated to improving network with each other, building friendships, supportng UC Santa Cruz's efforts towards broadening the universities' profile in China, and supporting each other world-wide. The CAA will also become the bridge between alumni and current students to support each other and succeed together.

We are always here waiting for all Chinese alumni to join us!
